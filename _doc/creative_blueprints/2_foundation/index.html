GlowDesk The Case for Change
The Problem

Salons lose revenue every day to broken operations

  • ✕ Appointments scattered across texts, calls, and paper books — double-bookings and empty chairs go unnoticed until revenue is already lost.
  • ✕ No-shows drain the schedule with no automated way to follow up or fill the slot in time.
  • ✕ Product inventory runs out mid-service; reorders are reactive, rushed, and costly to margins.
  • ✕ Clients drift away between visits because no one nudges them back at exactly the right moment.
The Solution

One platform. Full control over bookings, team, and inventory.

  • A visual appointment calendar with smart booking, chair assignment, and 24/7 online booking for clients.
  • Automated reminders cut no-shows; smart rebooking nudges bring clients back before they drift to a competitor.
  • Live inventory tracking with low-stock alerts so you never run out of product during a service.
  • A revenue dashboard that surfaces daily performance, chair utilization, and your top stylists at a glance.
