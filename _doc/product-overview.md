# GlowDesk — Product Overview

## What It Is
GlowDesk is a full-stack salon management platform built for beauty salon owners. It centralizes appointment scheduling, stylist workforce management, and product inventory tracking into one operational hub — purpose-built to maximize booking utilization and drive client retention.

## Who It's For
**Primary user: The salon owner** — typically a hands-on business operator who also works the floor. They need visibility across the whole business without leaving the chair.
**Secondary users:** Front-desk staff who manage daily bookings and client check-ins; stylists who need their own schedule and client notes at a glance.

## Core Value Proposition
A salon's revenue lives in two places: the appointment book and the returning client. GlowDesk is built to fill both — optimizing every chair hour and turning first-time visitors into regulars through smart scheduling, automated outreach, and client history that actually gets used.

## Key Features
1. **Appointment Scheduling** — Visual drag-and-drop calendar with chair/room assignment, service duration logic, and an online booking link clients can use 24/7.
2. **Stylist Management** — Stylist profiles with availability schedules, skill tags, booking load visibility, and per-stylist performance metrics (revenue generated, client retention rate).
3. **Product Inventory** — Track retail and backbar stock levels, set low-stock alerts, log product usage per service, and surface reorder recommendations before stockouts happen.
4. **Client CRM** — Per-client profiles with full service history, color formulas, notes, preferences, visit frequency, and lifetime value.
5. **Smart Reminders & Rebooking** — Automated SMS/email appointment reminders to cut no-shows; post-visit rebooking nudges timed to each client's average return interval.
6. **Revenue & Booking Dashboard** — Real-time view of daily/weekly revenue, chair utilization rate, upcoming booking gaps, and top-performing stylists.

## Positioning
- **Category:** Salon management SaaS / booking and operations platform
- **Differentiator:** Built specifically for beauty salons — not a generic scheduling tool. The dual focus on maximizing bookings AND retaining clients distinguishes GlowDesk from pure booking apps.
- **Tone:** Professional, warm, empowering. Speaks owner-to-owner — business-forward, never tech-heavy.
